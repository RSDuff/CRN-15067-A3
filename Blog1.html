Roast your own coffee in the pan

Probably the easiest way to roast your own coffee: spread raw coffee beans evenly on the bottom of a pan and heat it on the cooker, stirring constantly. After about 5 to 10 minutes, the beans start to "crack"; after about 20 minutes, even a second time.
